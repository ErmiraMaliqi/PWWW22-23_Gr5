Let's talk about your project

A decade of brand guardianship

Mint Creative has been Starbucks Australia's design partner for over 10 years, with work spanning print and digital channels. We take global direction and translate it to the Australian market.
This includes directing photoshoots, headline development, visual language development and print production whilst always maintaining global brand consistency.

The gift of strategic storytelling

Mint Creative worked with Clarins to create design concepts around the concept of giving, based on narratives that would position the brand as an ethical skincare company.
Three gift sets were created to target distinct audiences, with an emphasis on the brand's sustainably sourced ingredients, love of nature, and commitment to regenerating the planet.

A brand to reflect - and build - a legacy

Real estate agency Max Walls needed a new brand that both highlighted its strong and established history, yet positioned it as a modern business leading the industry.
Push Creative developed elegant and prestigious branding, brought to life across print and digital channels, including creative use of print textures and video.
